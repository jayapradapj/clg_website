Central Library

State-of-the-art library with an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources. Features quiet study spaces and modern research facilities.

Comfortable accommodation with modern amenities, 24/7 security, and dedicated mess facilities for both boys and girls.

A modern cafeteria serving a variety of nutritious meals, snacks, and beverages in a comfortable dining environment.

An on-campus medical facility with qualified healthcare professionals providing 24/7 emergency medical services.
