Bachelor of Performing Arts

The Bachelor of Performing Arts is a vibrant Programme crafted for students passionate about Classical and Contemporary Performance Arts. It offers specialized training in Dance, Music, and Theatre, Blending Rigorous Practice with Theoretical Knowledge of Aesthetics, Performance History, and Stagecraft.

Highlights
  • Professional training in Music, Dance, and Theatre
  • Exposure to Classical, Folk, and Contemporary Forms
  • Regular Performances, Festivals, and Competitions
  • Mentorship by accomplished Practitioners and Scholars
  • Career pathways in Arts Education, Media, and Stage
Eligibility
  • Completion of 10+2 (or equivalent) from a recognized board
  • Passion and Aptitude for Performing Arts
Career Opportunities
  • Performing Artists (Actors, Dancers, Musicians)
  • Choreographers & Directors
  • Music Composers & Vocal Trainers
  • Film & Theatre Professionals
B. Sc. Indology

B. Sc. Indology is a unique Interdisciplinary Course that delves deep into the study of Indian Civilization, Languages, Literature, Philosophy, Art, and Culture. It offers a comprehensive understanding of India's Ancient Texts, Archaeological Heritage, Spiritual Traditions, and Socio-Political Evolution.

Highlights
  • Interdisciplinary Curriculum blending History, Art, and Culture
  • Training in Ancient Indian Languages and Texts
  • Fieldwork and Heritage Site Documentation
  • Opportunities in Cultural Tourism, Research, and Museums
  • Emphasis on Critical Thinking and Scholarly Writing
Eligibility
  • Completion of 10+2 (or equivalent) from a recognized board
  • Interest in History, Culture, Philosophy and Heritage Studies
Career Opportunities
  • Archaeology & Heritage Management
  • Museology & Archival Studies
  • Tourism & Cultural Consultancy
  • Indigenous Knowledge-Based Industries
  • Publishing & Journalism
B. Sc. Radiology and Imaging Technology

B. Sc. Radiology and Imaging Technology is a Dynamic Programme designed to train students in Diagnostic Imaging Techniques such as X-ray, CT, MRI, and Ultrasound. The course blends Anatomy, Pathology, and Physics with practical instruction in using Advanced Radiological Equipment.

Highlights
  • Hands-on training in Advanced Imaging Technologies
  • Strong Clinical Exposure and Internship support
  • Focus on Patient Safety and Ethical Imaging Practices
  • Real-time Diagnostics with Modern Tools and Software
  • Employment in Hospitals, Diagnostic Labs, and Health Tech
Eligibility
  • Completion of 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics
  • Strong interest in Medical Technology and Patient Care
Career Opportunities
  • Hospitals & Specialized Labs across the World
  • International CT/MRI Technicians
  • Global Diagnostic Imaging Specialists
  • Radiation Safety Officers - Worldwide
B. Tech. Aviation & Drone Engineering

B. Tech. in Aviation & Drone Engineering is a specialized course that prepares students for careers in the fast-growing Aerospace and UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) Sectors. The Programme covers Aircraft Design, Flight Mechanics, Avionics, and Drone Technology.

Highlights
  • Advanced training in Aeronautical and Drone Technologies
  • Practical exposure to UAV Design and Control Systems
  • Flight Simulation, Prototyping and Testing Labs
  • Career Opportunities in Aerospace, Logistics and Defense
  • Compliance with Global Aviation Safety Standards
Eligibility
  • Completion of 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • Passion for Aviation, Technology and Robotics
Global Professional Opportunities
  • Aerospace & Aviation Industry
  • Defense & Security
  • Commercial Drone Sector
  • Research & Development
B. Tech. Artificial Intelligence & Medical Imaging

B. Tech. Programme combines the power of Artificial Intelligence with Medical Imaging Technologies to reshape Healthcare Diagnostics. Students learn AI Fundamentals, Deep Learning, Image Processing and Data Analytics, with applications in Radiology, Pathology and Smart Medical Systems.

Highlights
  • Integration of AI with real-time Medical Imaging Systems
  • Hands-on training using Advanced Healthcare Datasets
  • Research opportunities in Diagnostic Innovation
  • Strong Industry Collaboration with Healthcare Tech Firms
  • Career-ready for roles in AI Healthcare, med-tech, and R&D
Eligibility
  • Completion of 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • Strong interest in AI, Healthcare Technology, and Medical Imaging
Worldwide Career Opportunities
  • Healthcare AI Development
  • Medical Imaging Industry
  • Hospital & Diagnostic Centers
  • Research & Development
B. Sc. Medical Laboratory Technology

B.Sc. in Medical Laboratory Technology (MLT) Programme equips students with the knowledge and skills to conduct Clinical Laboratory Tests essential for Disease Detection, Diagnosis, and Treatment. Covering Biochemistry, Microbiology, Hematology, and Pathology, the Curriculum emphasizes Accuracy, Quality Control, and Lab Safety.

Highlights
  • In-depth Training In Pathology and Laboratory Diagnostics
  • Advanced Lab Infrastructure and Safety Protocols
  • Strong emphasis on Clinical Internships and Skill Labs
  • Opportunities in Hospitals, Research labs and Diagnostics
  • Quality Assurance and Ethical Practices in Diagnostics
Eligibility
  • Completion of 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Interest in Medical Diagnostics and Laboratory Sciences
Career Opportunities
  • QC/QA Professional in Multinational Industries
  • International Pathology Technicians
  • Clinical Laboratory Scientist
  • Public Health and Research Labs
