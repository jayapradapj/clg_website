Chancellor

Adv. Dr. P. Krishnadas

Welcome to P K DAS Institute of Social Sciences, Health Sciences and Technology a Deemed to be University under District Category, driven by a vision to nurture minds, transform lives, and shape a better future.

We offer a vibrant learning environment that integrates the rigour of science and technology with the compassion of Social Sciences and Healthcare. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure, distinguished faculty, and dynamic curriculum ensure that students receive not only knowledge but also the values and skills needed to become global leaders, empathetic caregivers, and change-makers. Our Interdisciplinary and Multidisciplinary approach allow the students to explore diverse fields while remaining grounded in ethical practices and community engagement. We encourage Innovation, Research, and real-world problem-solving, ensuring that our graduates are Industry-ready and socially conscious, to fit into any International Segment.

As Chancellor, I do take pride in seeing our students evolve into confident and competent professionals who make meaningful contributions to global society. At P K Das University, you will not just earn a Degree you will ignite a purpose, find your voice and build a legacy. Join us in this journey of learning and transformation. Together, let us create a future that reflects our shared commitment to knowledge, humanity and progress along with all developments at International Scenario.
